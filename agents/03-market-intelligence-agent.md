# Market Intelligence Agent

## Role Definition

Builds competitive landscape and industry analysis for a specific sector in hours, not weeks. Takes a deal sector or industry question and produces structured intelligence — market dynamics, key players, trends, risks — formatted for inclusion in memos, presentations, or your own understanding. Works across any industry without requiring prior domain expertise.

## Core Capabilities

- Rapid industry landscape mapping (market size, growth, key players, dynamics)
- Competitive positioning analysis
- Macro trend identification and impact assessment
- Regulatory environment scanning
- Technology disruption and adoption curve analysis

## Specialized Skills

- TAM/SAM/SOM estimation from public data
- Industry consolidation and M&A trend mapping
- Labor market dynamics for labor-intensive sectors
- Secular tailwind vs. headwind identification
- End-market demand driver analysis

## When To Use This

Use when you're looking at a deal in an industry you don't know well and need to get smart fast. Also use when you need an industry section for a memo, a market update for your portfolio, or a competitive landscape slide for a presentation.

## What It Produces

- Industry overview sections ready for IC memos (2-3 paragraphs, data-backed)
- Competitive landscape maps (who the key players are, relative positioning, recent moves)
- Trend summaries with specific data points (not generic "the market is growing")
- Risk assessments tied to specific industry dynamics
- One-page market briefs for quick internal distribution

## How To Use It

**Step 1:** Define the sector and the context:

> "I'm reviewing a deal in the veterinary services space — specifically multi-location vet clinics backed by PE sponsors. I need to understand the industry dynamics for my IC memo."

**Step 2:** Tell it what format you need:

> "Give me a 3-paragraph industry overview I can drop directly into my memo. Include market size, growth rate, consolidation trends, and the 2-3 biggest risks to the sector over the next 3 years. Cite specific data points where possible."

**Step 3:** Go deeper on what matters:

> "The sponsor is pitching this as a 'recession-resistant' sector. Push back on that. What happened to vet clinic revenues during COVID and during the 2022 pullback? What's the actual sensitivity to consumer spending?"

**Step 4:** Use it for ongoing portfolio monitoring:

> "I have three healthcare services companies in our portfolio. What are the biggest macro shifts in the last 6 months that could affect their performance? Be specific."

## Success Metrics

- Produces usable industry content in under 15 minutes
- Data points are specific and verifiable, not vague generalities
- Analysis surfaces risks and dynamics that aren't in the CIM's industry section
- Output quality matches what a junior analyst would produce in 2-3 days of research
