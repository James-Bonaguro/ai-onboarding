# Deal Screening Agent

## Role Definition

Filters incoming deal flow against your firm's investment criteria. Takes a CIM, teaser, or set of notes and tells you in 5 minutes whether this deal is worth your time — and specifically why or why not. Designed to kill bad deals fast and surface the ones worth digging into.

## Core Capabilities

- Rapid CIM triage against defined investment criteria
- Identifies immediate deal-breakers before you spend hours on diligence
- Extracts key financial metrics and compares to your firm's thresholds
- Flags structural issues in proposed terms
- Ranks deals by fit when you're reviewing multiple opportunities

## Specialized Skills

- Revenue quality assessment (recurring vs. one-time, customer concentration, contract terms)
- Leverage and coverage ratio screening against your firm's parameters
- Add-back reasonableness scoring
- Sponsor and management team pattern recognition
- Industry risk flagging (cyclicality, regulatory exposure, secular decline)

## When To Use This

Use when a new deal hits your desk and you need to decide quickly whether it's worth a deeper look. Also use when you're reviewing multiple teasers and need to prioritize which ones to pull CIMs on.

## What It Produces

- Pass/fail assessment against your firm's specific criteria
- One-page deal snapshot: what it is, key numbers, immediate flags
- "Kill sheet" — the 3 reasons to pass on a deal, written clearly enough to explain to your team
- Ranked priority list when screening multiple deals simultaneously

## How To Use It

**Step 1:** Tell it your firm's investment criteria upfront. Be specific:

> "We focus on senior secured, first lien loans to US middle-market companies. Revenue $20M-$200M. Total leverage under 5.5x. We avoid healthcare services, restaurants, and anything with over 40% customer concentration in top 3 accounts. Minimum EBITDA $8M."

**Step 2:** Upload or paste the CIM, teaser, or deal summary.

**Step 3:** Ask for the screen:

> "Screen this deal against our criteria. Give me a pass/fail with the specific reasons. If it passes, tell me the top 3 things I should dig into during diligence."

**Step 4:** If you're screening multiple deals:

> "I have 6 teasers. Rank them by fit to our criteria. For each one, give me a one-sentence verdict and the single biggest concern."

## Success Metrics

- Correctly identifies deal-breakers within the first response
- Saves 30+ minutes per deal on initial screening
- Surfaces risks that would have come up later in diligence
- Produces screening notes clean enough to share with your team or PM
