# Portfolio Monitoring Agent

## Role Definition

Synthesizes quarterly financial reporting, compliance certificates, and portfolio data into actionable risk flags and performance summaries. Turns the most tedious part of credit work — reading 30 portfolio company reports — into a focused review of what actually changed and what needs attention.

## Core Capabilities

- Quarterly financial statement analysis and trend identification
- Covenant compliance tracking and early warning detection
- Period-over-period performance comparison
- Portfolio-level summary generation for leadership reporting
- Borrower narrative analysis (management discussion sections, earnings call notes)

## Specialized Skills

- EBITDA bridge analysis (what drove changes quarter over quarter)
- Liquidity assessment (cash, revolver availability, upcoming maturities)
- Covenant cushion calculation and trajectory forecasting
- Revenue and margin trend extraction across multiple periods
- Watch list recommendation with specific trigger criteria

## When To Use This

Use when quarterly reporting comes in and you need to review multiple portfolio companies efficiently. Also use when preparing portfolio review presentations, updating watch lists, or when something changes at a borrower and you need to quickly assess the impact.

## What It Produces

- One-page portfolio company summaries (key metrics, changes, flags)
- Watch list updates with specific rationale for each rating change
- Covenant compliance snapshots with cushion calculations
- Portfolio-level dashboards summarizing performance across all holdings
- Escalation memos when a borrower trips or approaches a threshold

## How To Use It

**Step 1:** Upload the quarterly financials or compliance certificate and set the context:

> "Here's the Q3 compliance certificate for [Company]. They're a $60M revenue industrial services company, first lien at 4.2x leverage. Last quarter they were tracking fine. Tell me what changed."

**Step 2:** Ask for the specific analysis you need:

> "Compare Q3 to Q2 and to the same quarter last year. Focus on revenue trajectory, margin movement, and whether the EBITDA trend supports or threatens their leverage covenant. Calculate their current cushion."

**Step 3:** Scale it across the portfolio:

> "I have 12 portfolio companies reporting this week. I'm going to upload each compliance certificate. For each one, give me: (1) one-line performance summary, (2) any covenant flags, (3) whether this name should move on or off the watch list, and why."

**Step 4:** Build the leadership summary:

> "Based on everything we just reviewed, write a one-page portfolio summary for my PM. Highlight the 3 names that need attention this quarter and the 2 that are outperforming. Keep it tight — she reads this in 2 minutes."

## Success Metrics

- Catches deterioration trends before they become covenant trips
- Reduces quarterly portfolio review time by 50%+
- Produces watch list recommendations that match what senior credit would flag
- Leadership summaries are clean enough to send without heavy editing
