# Rule 2: Right Now, Your AI Knows Nothing About You

## The Problem

Open your AI right now. It has no idea:

- What your job is
- What industry you're in
- How senior you are
- What your firm's standards look like
- What kind of work you actually produce

It's like hiring someone and never telling them what company they work for. Of course the output is generic — you never told it anything about you.

## The Fix

Every AI tool lets you store background information about yourself. This runs silently behind every conversation. Once you set it up, every single output improves automatically.

**In ChatGPT:** Settings → Personalization → Custom Instructions
**In Claude:** Settings → Profile → Custom Instructions

You fill this in once. It takes 15 minutes. Then it works forever.

## What To Put In There

Here's what your AI needs to know about you. Not all of this will apply — use what fits:

**Who you are:**
- Your title and role
- Your industry and firm type
- How many years of experience you have
- What kinds of decisions you make vs. what you hand to someone else

**What good work looks like:**
- Your firm's communication style (formal? direct? data-heavy?)
- The formats you use most (memos, models, emails, decks)
- What your boss or your committee cares about most

**What to avoid:**
- Generic corporate language
- Overly long responses when you need something concise
- Agreeing with everything you say (more on this in Rule 5)

## Before vs. After

**Before setup (default AI):**
> You: "Help me with this deal memo."
> AI: "Sure! Here's a general template for a deal memorandum. A deal memo typically includes an executive summary, company overview, financial analysis..."

**After setup (configured AI):**
> You: "Help me with this deal memo."
> AI: "Based on your firm's IC format, I'll structure this as: (1) Investment Thesis with the key return drivers, (2) Business Quality assessment, (3) Key Risks with mitigants, (4) Preliminary Terms. Want me to start with the thesis based on what you've shared, or do you want to upload the CIM first?"

Same question. Wildly different output. The only thing that changed is that the AI knows who it's talking to.

## The One-Sentence Rule

**The more your AI knows about you and your work, the less time you spend fixing its output.**

Set it up once. Benefit forever.
